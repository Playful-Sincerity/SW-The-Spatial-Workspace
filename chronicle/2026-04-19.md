## 21:00 — Viewport sync + collapse fit-to-view bug

**What:** All bulk ops (tier +/-, expand-all, menu expand/collapse one tier + fully) now call `fitToView()`. Discovered a bug: collapse was zooming *out* instead of *in*, because `fitToView` was reading `g.getBBox()` mid-animation — exit-selection nodes still sat at their far-out old positions. Fixed by computing the bbox from `state.layoutRoot.descendants()` (target positions) instead of the live DOM. Also tied the viewport transition duration to `ANIM_DURATION + 80` so the zoom lands just after the layout settles (was a fixed 320ms — too fast and desynced).
**Why:** Wisdom wanted "whenever the whole thing expands at once, always zooms out to make sure the whole expanse is within the canvas" — then noticed collapse wasn't honoring it, then noticed collapse zoom felt faster than expand. Three distinct observations, one underlying cause (viewport timing + data source).
**Means:** `fitToView` prefers layout-data bbox with DOM fallback. All bulk-op paths now `requestAnimationFrame(() => fitToView())` after `updateTree`.
**Branch:** `main`
**Files:** `templates/v3-bubble/app.js`
