# RenMap Lives Inside Spatial Workspace

Spatial Workspace is the container. RenMap is an application within it.

RenMap works from people's Digital Core — their own knowledge, connections, interests. It's a way to view people and things and everything they're interested in with localizability (spatial positioning). The spatial interface is what makes the social layer navigable rather than feed-based.

## Build Order
1. Build Spatial Workspace as the platform
2. Build RenMap as an app inside it
3. Can ship RenMap as a standalone external app first, but the real power comes from the Spatial Workspace integration

## Why This Matters
Spatial Workspace is "the missing core" — the environment where everything lives. Without it, RenMap is just another social network. With it, RenMap becomes a spatial lens on real knowledge and real relationships, powered by each person's Digital Core.

## Implication
This reframes the relationship: Spatial Workspace isn't just a document/system visualization tool — it's the platform layer. RenMap, and potentially other PS Software projects, become applications within it.
