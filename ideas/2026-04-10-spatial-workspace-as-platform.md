# Spatial Workspace — The Platform Layer (Summary)

Three ideas crystallized on 2026-04-10 that reframe Spatial Workspace from "visualization tool" to "the platform everything lives in":

1. **RenMap is an app inside Spatial Workspace** — social/people layer powered by each person's Digital Core
   → See: `2026-04-10-renmap-inside-spatial-workspace.md`

2. **n8n proves the canvas-based workflow model** — Spatial Workspace extends it by making humans first-class nodes alongside agents and automations
   → See: `2026-04-10-n8n-spatial-workspace-convergence.md`

3. **Contribution tracking is automatic** — if people work within the spatial workspace, their activity IS the contribution record
   → See: `~/Playful Sincerity/PS Stewardship/governance-economics/ideas/2026-04-10-digital-core-as-contribution-engine.md`

## The Full Stack

```
Spatial Workspace (environment)
├── RenMap (social — people, interests, locality)
├── Project Management (workflows — humans + agents + automations)
├── Digital Core (knowledge — chronicles, memory, rules, skills)
└── Contribution Tracking (governance — automatic from all above)
```

Spatial Workspace is the missing core.
